Hello! My name is Marlynn Garcia, and I am a passionate Full Stack Developer based in Texas. My journey into tech is fueled by a deep love for problem-solving and innovation. Before diving into software engineering, I had a rewarding career as a Microbiologist, working on groundbreaking infectious disease research projects. My path took an exciting turn when I began teaching at a high school dedicated to empowering a minority community through enhanced educational opportunities, in partnership with a local community college. It was during this time that my enthusiasm for STEM and technology truly ignited, inspiring me to transition into the world of software engineering. I am thrilled to merge my scientific background with my tech skills, bringing a unique perspective to the field. My dedication to learning and growing as a developer is unwavering, and I am eager to contribute to and collaborate with others in this vibrant and ever-evolving industry.
